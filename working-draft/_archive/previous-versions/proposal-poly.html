◆ Current Situation

What We Observed

Based on Avanti's digital presence, menu, reviews, and operating data.

Digital Presence Isolated

Avanti's website exists but operates separately from ordering. Menu, social, and site don't connect into a single sales flow, making it hard for customers to find, choose, and order directly.

Aggregator Dependency & Fee Leakage

Without a direct-order channel, Avanti relies on aggregators that take significant margin. Every third-party order reduces effective revenue per dish without building any long-term relationship.

Customer Retention Gap

Previous customers have no way to reorder easily, track loyalty, or stay connected. Returning customers are the highest-margin revenue stream — but the system doesn't encourage repeat visits.

Menu & Pricing Visibility

Avanti's full menu with accurate pricing isn't easily accessible across touchpoints. Customers browsing online can't immediately see what's available, what it costs, or how to order.
